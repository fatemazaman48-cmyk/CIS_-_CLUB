About CIS Club

Learn more about who we are and what we do

Our Story

At Daffodil International University, our clubs and organizations are the heart of campus life, fostering a vibrant and inclusive community. From academic and professional development to arts, culture, and sports, our diverse range of clubs offers something for everyone. Get involved, make connections, and enrich your university experience with us.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of continuous learning, innovation, and collaboration among students interested in computer science and information technology. We strive to provide platforms for skill development, knowledge sharing, and professional growth.

Our Vision

To become the leading student organization that empowers future tech leaders through excellence in education, innovation, and community engagement. We envision a community where every member can achieve their full potential.
