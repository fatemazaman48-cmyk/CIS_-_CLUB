Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the CIS Club?

You can join by filling out the membership form on our website or visiting our office during office hours. Membership is open to all students interested in computer science.

Are there membership fees?

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee of $20 which covers event participation, workshops, and club materials.

Do I need programming experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced programmers. We have activities suitable for everyone.

How often do you organize events?

We typically organize 2-3 major events per month, including workshops, competitions, and seminars. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.
